News and Observations

  • Irving maintained retained La Liga championship for yet another season.

  • However, Alfredo and Connor came from behind to spectacularly reach second and third place, respectively, edging out Sebastien and Genaro into 4th and 5th, respectively, in what has been one of the closest campaigns in years.

  • Connor won the second leg of the cup final.

  • For the first time in the cup history, a tiebreaker had to be used for the final, where Irving totaled 16 points in the bench vs Connor's 8 points, making Irving the winner.

  • Connor tallied the best rookie season seen in the tournament history: he reached top three in La Liga, won top seed in his group, and tied in the Cup's final. Well done!

  • Alfredo put a fight in La Liga and reached second place while also winning Pichichi trophy for winning the most matchdays.

  • And as always #GenarofortheSacko2022, see you next season! Thanks for participating!
